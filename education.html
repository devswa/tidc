University Setup Policy

Directorate

Department of Higher Education

Land Assistance

1. For 1-20 NIRF ranked Institutions 100 % permium free land
2. For 21-50 NIRF ranked Institutions 75 % permium free land
3. For 51-100 NIRF ranked Institutions 50 % permium free land

Elgibility

  • Running atleast 2 Colleges/higher Education institues for about 10Years.
  • Established Endownment Fund of Rs. 1 Cr.
  • atleast 1 Prof/ 1 Asst. Prof should be in Managing body.
  • Minimum Built up area 1 Lakhs sq ft.
  • Society under society Registration Act ,1860 , public trust registered under state public trust Act or India Trust Act 1882.
  • Company Registered under sector 8 company act 2013,
  • 3rd Party is allow to Contribute .
  • Minimum 20 Acres of land outsite of Municipal Limits
  • 5 Acres within Municipal limits
